A Generational Shift

The Dawn of the Great SME M&A Era

A profound demographic shift is creating a massive, once-in-a-generation market opportunity. In both Japan and Korea, millions of successful first-generation SME founders are reaching retirement age, creating an urgent need for succession solutions. This isn't a niche problem; it's a vast and rapidly growing M&A market driven by powerful, irreversible trends.

Demographic Imperative

The "baby boomer" generation of founders is retiring. With fewer family members taking over, M&A has become the primary and most logical path for business succession.

Economic Value at Stake

These are not failing companies. They are profitable, stable businesses that form the backbone of the economy. Their transition represents a multi-trillion KRW opportunity to transfer and unlock immense economic value.

A New Class of Buyers

The market is expanding beyond traditional corporate acquirers. A new wave of individual entrepreneurs (ETA) and private capital is actively seeking to acquire and operate these quality SMEs.

Our Matching Technology

The AI M&A Manager

Our true differentiation is our AI's ability to identify "Strategic Fit," going beyond financials to deliver not just more buyers, but better buyers who will be responsible stewards of a company's legacy.

The AI automates the creation of professional teaser notes, a task that traditionally consumes weeks of an analyst's time. The process is interactive, engaging with sellers to refine and enrich the data for maximum impact.

Our algorithm performs a deep analysis of a potential buyer's strategic objectives, existing business lines, and technology portfolio. It identifies partners based on post-merger synergies, technological complementarity, and long-term strategic alignment.

The AI extracts a curated list of the most suitable potential buyers from our extensive database. Each candidate is scored and ranked based on the calculated probability of a successful strategic match.

Once a high-potential match is identified and validated, the AI hands off the opportunity to our expert human managers, who facilitate the next phase of discussions, blending high-tech scale with high-touch expertise.

Building a Sustainable Buyer Pipeline

The Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition (ETA) Program

A healthy M&A market requires a constant supply of qualified buyers. To solve this, we created Korea's leading ETA program, actively cultivating a new generation of entrepreneurs to become the next owners of great SMEs.
